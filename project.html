Videos Games worth trying in 2021

Reviews and Summary of Video Games, Each Game with be given a score rated out of 10.

Video Games List

4.5 - NBA 2k21 is basketball Franchise that evoles over the years through the NBA seasons.This game is time consuming and takes awhile to grind out your character and this game is fun to play with friends. This game has issues that are game breaking and can ruin the experience.


8.5 - Fighterz is a competitive fighting game in a 2d based arena, the graphics are visually stunning and the animations are smooth.


9 - Rocket League is a free to play game based around soccer with cars and is fun to play with friends.


8.3 - InFamous Second Son is a great game, the gameplay is great and the wide open sections of the game are fun to explore. The story mode is short and the side missions shallow and doesn't develop any storyline.

9 - Marvel's Spiderman by Insominac is a great game and a immersive experience. The webslinging in the game is smooth and nostagic to a previous spdierman game.
